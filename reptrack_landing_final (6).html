Common questions

Does RepTrack work with my IRCC login?

Yes. RepTrack runs entirely inside your browser using your existing IRCC representative portal login. We never store or see your credentials or client data.

How many applications can it scan?

RepTrack can scan your entire representative queue regardless of size. Firms with 2,000+ applications typically complete a full scan in 30-40 minutes.

Where are the PDFs saved?

PDFs are downloaded directly to your computer's Downloads folder, named with the date, application number and applicant name for easy filing.

Can multiple team members use it?

Yes. Your license covers your whole team. Anyone with the license key can install and use RepTrack. The annual plan includes unlimited team members.

What happens when IRCC updates their portal?

We monitor IRCC portal changes and push updates automatically. Your extension updates itself — no action needed from you.

Do I need a contract?

No contracts, no setup fees. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. Annual plans are billed once per year.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account. No cancellation fees or long-term contracts.
